SRM University Admissions

The SRM University generally conducts its entrance exam known as SRMJEEE and SRMCAT for admission to engineering and management Courses respectively. Admission to SRM UG medical courses is on the basis of marks scored in NEET Exam. The admission to other UG and PG courses is usually merit-based. The application process for SRM University is both online and offline.

Steps to Apply:

  1. Visit the official website of the SRM University.
  2. Register and login with the provided email ID and password.
  3. Complete the application form and attach the necessary details such as scanned copy of signature, coloured photograph, class X marksheet & XII marksheet (size not more than 500 kb).
  4. Successful submission of application form will depend upon the payment of application fee.
  5. For online registration or downloaded forms, candidates need to pay INR 1100 either through e-payment or DD.
  6. However, the cost for the offline application form obtained from the university office is INR 1160.
  7. Upon successful submission of the application form, students need to save their application number for future reference.

Note: The application fee for SRM University application form can be paid both online and offline. Online payment can be done by using a valid debit and credit card. In case of offline payment, it can be done through a DD drawn in favor of SRMIST, Payable at Chennai.
