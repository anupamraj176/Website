DSCE Admission Process

The college offers a facility of an online application process for all programmes. Candidates, who meet the given eligibility criteria, need to register and fill an application form to apply for a particular programme. The course-wise details regarding DSCE admissions are mentioned below:

  • BE admission is based on JEE Main/COMEDK UGET/KCET score. For lateral entry admissions, CET scores are considered
  • Admission to MTech programme is based on GATE/Karnataka PGCET score
  • Candidates willing to seek MBA/MCA admission must have a valid score in Karnataka PGCET/MAT/CAT/KMAT
  • For admission to the PhD/MSc (research) programme, VTU conducts ULRAT entrance test

DSCE Placements

DSCE has a placement cell that coordinates with recruiting companies and handles the entire end-to-end placement process. It is provided with a well-equipped auditorium for organising pre-placement talks and well-furnished rooms for training, interviews and counselling. The cell works closely with the final year students and companies hiring fresher students. It is also committed to enhance the employability skills of the students by organising training programmes, including aptitude training programme, soft-skills training, subject/domain-specific training and entrepreneurship development.
