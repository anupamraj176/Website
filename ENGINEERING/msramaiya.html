About MS Ramaiah Institute of Technology

Located in Bengaluru, Ramaiah Institute of Technology (RIT) is affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), which is headquartered in Belgaum. Set up in the year 1962, this autonomous private engineering college has been offering postgraduate and undergraduate programmes since August 2007. Although, VTU awards degrees, the college is free to frame its curricula and schemes of study. The college offers various undergraduate programmes in diverse streams like civil engineering, mechanical engineering, chemical engineering, biotechnology, and medical electronics. It also offers MCA and MTech in different streams, in addition to providing research programmes in various departments that lead to PhD/MSc (Engineering).

Ramaiah Institute of Technology Admission Process 2025

Students seeking admission in Bachelor of Engineering and Bachelor of Architecture programmes at RIT need to clear KEA/COMEDK (Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka) entrance exam. However, the eligibility criteria for enrollment in BE programme at RIT is securing at least 45% in 10+2 examinations with mathematics and physics as the compulsory subjects. In addition, students should have passed 10+2 exams with 50% in mathematics, physics, and chemistry, apart from securing 50% in aggregate at the 10+2 level. For admission to the MBA programme, students need to appear for KMAT/CMAT/MAT/PGCET, while for MCA, students should have cleared KMAT or PGCET. For MTech, students need to have cleared GATE or should have appeared for PGCET of Karnataka. The eligibility criteria for admission to MBA and MCA programmes are 3-year bachelor's degree from a recognized institute and a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.
