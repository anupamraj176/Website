RVCE Bangalore: Admission Process

The application process for admissions to BTech commences with the online registrations for JEE Mains/KCET (for Karnataka candidates)/COMEDK (for outside Karnataka candidates), and the application process for admissions to PG courses commences with the online registrations for GATE/PGCET. The application fee can be paid via Bank Challan or Demand Draft (drawn in favor of Executive Director Karnataka Examinations Authority, payable at Bangalore).

Admissions to all the programme is based on the scores obtained in the required entrance exam, followed by the Counseling. Interested candidates can refer to below mentioned details about RVCE admission process:

  • Admission to BTech programme is based on the scores obtained in KCET 2025/COMEDK 2025/ JEE Mains 2024
  • For the Karnataka residing candidates, the selection to BE course is done on the basis of KCET 2025 (Karnataka Common Entrance Test) conducted by Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA)
  • For the non-Karnataka residing candidates, the selection of the candidates to BE course is done on the basis of marks scored in COMEDK 2025 examination
  • 45% seats are filled through KCET, 30% seats are filled based on COMEDK 2025 and 25% seats are reserved for Management, all merit-based quota
  • 80% seats are reserved for students appearing in GATE 2024 and PGCET 2025 exam and 20% seats are reserved for Management Quota
  • 20% of seats of the MCA programme are given to those applying in Lateral Entry through KEA counseling

Eligibility Criteria:

To qualify for management quota admission at RV College of Engineering, you must satisfy the following requirements:

  • Completion of 10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized board with at least 45% aggregate in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • A valid score in any of the following entrance exams: COMEDK, JEE Main, KCET, or the equivalent exam
  • Age limit criteria as specified by the college

Admission Process:

The following steps are involved in securing management quota admission at RV College of Engineering:

  • Fill in the application form for management quota admission, which is available on the college website
  • Submit the necessary documents, such as your 10+2 mark sheet, entrance exam scorecard, age proof, and identity proof, along with the application form
  • The college authorities will review your academic performance and other eligibility criteria upon receiving your application
  • If you meet the eligibility criteria, you will be invited for a personal interview with the college authorities
  • The selection of candidates for management quota admission will be based on their academic performance and personal interview

Benefits of Management Quota Admission:

Here are some of the benefits of securing admission through management quota:

  • Guaranteed admission to RV College of Engineering without the need for rigorous entrance examinations
  • Limited seats under the management quota offer a better chance of securing a seat
  • By avoiding the entrance exam preparation, you can devote more time and effort to your academic performance

Conclusion:

In conclusion, securing management quota admission at RV College of Engineering is a viable option for students who are unable to meet the rigorous eligibility criteria for the regular admission process. With the limited number of seats under the management quota, candidates have a higher chance of securing a seat. By focusing on academic performance instead of entrance exam preparation, students can save time and effort while still ensuring a place in the prestigious institution. If you meet the eligibility criteria, you can apply for management quota admission by following the application process specified by the college.
